About the PRMSU Nursing Course

Our Nursing Course is designed to prepare students for a rewarding career in healthcare. With comprehensive theoretical knowledge and practical skills, our program aims to equip graduates to provide compassionate and effective patient care.

Curriculum Highlights

Fundamentals of Nursing

Introduction to nursing principles, ethics, and patient care fundamentals.

Medical-Surgical Nursing

In-depth knowledge of care for patients with various medical conditions.

Pediatric Nursing

Specialized care techniques for infants, children, and adolescents.

Community Health

Focus on public health, prevention, and community-based care strategies.

Admission Process

To apply for our Nursing Course, prospective students should submit an application form along with academic transcripts and a statement of purpose. Entrance exams and interviews are part of the selection process.
